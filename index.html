About Neon Dash

Welcome to Neon Dash, a fast-paced arcade browser game designed to test your reflexes. The goal is simple: avoid the falling neon blocks. As you progress, the speed increases, challenging your reaction time. This game is built with HTML5 and requires no downloads.

How to Play

On desktop, use your keyboard's Left and Right arrow keys to steer the ball. On mobile devices, simply tap the left or right side of the game area to move. Survive as long as you can to set a new high score!
